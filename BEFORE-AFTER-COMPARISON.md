### AFTER (New Section Added)

Quick FAQ

Q: When should I use the Hat Model vs. traditional tools?
A: Use hats when decisions require multiple perspectives, judgment calls,
   or cross-functional coordination. Use tools when your plugin is
   single-purpose and doesn't need to interact with others.

Q: How do I know if my Hat design is working?
A: Track these metrics: fewer manual invocations per decision (target: 1-2
   auto triggers vs. 4-6 manual), lower decision latency, higher escalation
   accuracy, and user feedback ("Does this feel like a colleague?").

Q: What's Layer 3 (Judgment) really about?
A: Position-taking. A hat doesn't just present options—it recommends based
   on its domain expertise and organizational context. This is what makes a
   plugin feel like a trusted colleague instead of a passive tool.

- BEFORE: No FAQ; users have to infer answers from prose
